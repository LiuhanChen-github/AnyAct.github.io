What is AnyAct?

AnyAct reenacts human motion from arbitrary character videos. It converts sparse 2D motion cues from animals, cartoons, objects, and stylized characters into plausible 3D human motions that preserve the source action semantics.

Method Overview

AnyAct uses sparse local 2D controls extracted from a source video to guide motion generation. The model is trained in two stages: a 3D stage learns sparse-motion guidance, and a 2D stage then learns to control generation from projected sparse 2D observations.
